Resnick Center Alumni Tracking System - Capstone Project Fall 2022

Our capstone project is for the Resnick Center for Student Athelete Alumni Tracking after they have graduated from Temple. This semester has focused on the analysis and design phases of the capstone process and we are working on getting to the designing portion of the system in the coming weeks. System will allow the employees at the Resnick Center full access to records on alumni information and engagement in order to provide more opportunities and build connections for the newer undergraduate student athletes. The product vision statement we are following goes, "For the professional development staff members of the Resnick Academic Support Center, who need a system to manage and track student athlete alumni information, the Resnick Alumni Tracking System is a web-based system that will facilitate alumni information gathering and management based on major, sport played, graduating year, and employment & education history. Different from the current system of excel spreadsheets, our web-based system allows for a more efficient process to update and manage alumni information within a centralized system.

Individual Project - Mental Health Wellness App - MntalMonitor

MntalMonitor is my personal project and I dedicated it to mental health since I feel as though students don't have many options when it comes to trying to better themselves mentally. The product vision statement is, "For people with little to none access to mental health resources, and looking for a way to keep their mental health and mood more manageable. MntalMonitor is amobile-based application that will store user entry data, and allow the user to obtain resources based on their inputs. Unlike a traditional journal, MntalMontior will provide users with lots of established resources in order to help them understand exactly what they are going through. Provides an hub app for people struggling to obtain help when severely needed.

CIS 3344 Final Project - Movie Review Application - Fall 2021

Me and my partner had designed a web-application that would allow users to post reviews for movies with a preset of the top 25 highest grossing movies as a preset list for all users. The list was its own database file and allowed for users to download the database for external use. Project was written in JavaScript and we were instructed to learn a new framework, and our group was given Vue.js. Vue.js allowed us to manipulate the images of the movies more freely and allowed for a cleaner layout.
